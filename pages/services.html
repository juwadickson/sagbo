Our French Classes & Services

Explore our range of personalized French learning experiences designed for all levels.

Individual Coaching

One-on-One Lessons: Personalized instruction tailored to the student's specific needs and goals.

Conversation Practice: Guided practice in speaking French fluently and confidently.

Grammar and Vocabulary Review: Focused sessions to strengthen language fundamentals.

Exam Preparation: Intensive training for French language exams like IGCSE, IBDP, DELF/DALF, or TEF/TCF.

Group Classes

Small Group Instruction: Interactive learning in a supportive environment.

Language Exchange: Practice French with native speakers.

Conversation Clubs: Casual gatherings to practice speaking French.

Online Learning

Virtual Classes: Live, interactive lessons conducted via video conferencing.

Recorded Lessons: Prerecorded video or audio lessons for flexible learning.

Online Resources: Access to digital materials, exercises, and quizzes.

Specialized Services

Business French: Coaching for professional needs, including correspondence, presentations, and meetings.

Travel French: Preparation for French-speaking destinations, covering key topics.

French for Children: Fun and engaging lessons for young learners.

French for Seniors: Customized instruction for older learners.

Additional Services

Cultural Immersion: Experience French culture through films, music, and cuisine.

Translation and Proofreading: Professional services for documents and content.

French Language Camps: Intensive immersion programs in French language and culture.
